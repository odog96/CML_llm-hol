Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement. On August 5, 2024, NCR Voyix Corporation NCR Voyix or the Company entered into a purchase agreement the Purchase Agreement with Dragon Buyer, Inc. Buyer, an affiliate of The Veritas Capital Fund VIII, L.P. Veritas, pursuant to which Buyer agreed to purchase the Companys digital banking business the Digital Banking Business. The purchase price for the transaction is 2.45 billion in cash, subject to a customary postclosing purchase price adjustment, as well as contingent consideration of up to an additional 100 million in cash upon the achievement of a specified return on Veritas and its affiliates invested capital at the time of any future sale. The transaction is structured as a purchase by Buyer of all of the equity interests of the Companys whollyowned subsidiary Digital First Holdings LLC Digital First and each subsidiary of Digital First following certain preclosing restructuring actions.
